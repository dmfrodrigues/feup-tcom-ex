A strategy to implement a TM to answer this question would be:
\begin{enumerate}
	\item \label{itm:tm-2} Go right until you find the first $x$, replace it by $X$ and go left until you find the first $0$/$1$.
	\item Increment this number: if $0$ or $B$, replace by $1$ and you're done; if $1$, replace by $0$, go left and do this step again. Once you're done, go to step \ref{itm:tm-2}.
\end{enumerate}
